The App Store is a treasure trove of apps. Here are some of the apps that I can't live without:

- [Amie](http://amie.so/) - better calendar app with to dos
- [Arc Search ](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/arc-search-ai-browser/id6472513080) - improved browser experience with AI summarization
- [Cosmos](https://www.cosmos.so/) - better pinterest
- [Language Transfer](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/language-transfer/id1519599004) - learning languages by relating them to english
- [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) - general note taking
- [Retro](https://retro.app/) - daily pictures
- [Spotify](https://www.spotify.com/my-en/premium/) - listening to music
- [Untitled](https://testflight.apple.com/join/a4bmx5rA) - minimal podcast app with no ads
- [Notes](https://www.icloud.com/notes) - short notes
- [Camp](https://apps.apple.com/my/app/camp-a-scrapbook/id1658539800) - classify each of your screenshots into different categories
- [Journal](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/journal/id6447391597) - daily journaling
